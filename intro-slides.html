R/Finance 2018: June 1st-2nd at UIC

From the inaugural conference in 2009, the annual R/Finance conference in Chicago has become the primary meeting for academics and practioners interested in using R in Finance. Participants from academia and industry mingle for two days to exchange ideas about current research, best practices and applications.

R Consortium News

This week, R Consortium named R-Ladies a top level project!

In 2016, R-Ladies had 4 chapters: San Francisco, Taipei, Twin Cities and London.

As of March 2018, R-Ladies has over 90 chapters, including Chicago, and almost 19,000 members!

The diversified voice of R-Ladies, speaking not only as a group representing gender minorities in tech but also a group attracting new R users, aligned with the R Consortiums defined Code of Conduct and its desires for building a more diverse and inclusive R community.

The R Consortium invite all in the R community to congratulate R-Ladies on this milestone.
